Dragon Ball Xenoverse Tag Team - PPSSPP MOD

This is a modded version of Dragon Ball Z: Tenkaichi Tag Team featuring a complete overhaul with Dragon Ball Xenoverse 2 UI, custom shaders, enhanced textures, and a special save file for unlocked content. Play on your Android using the PPSSPP emulator. No root required.
